Features
📦Products

Images, SKUs, categories, pricing, and stock levels with search and filtering.

🏭Multi-Warehouse

Track and transfer stock across multiple locations and warehouses.

📋Purchase Orders

Create POs, receive goods, and track supplier deliveries end-to-end.

🚚Sales Orders

Create and ship SOs. Auto-generate PDF invoices with embedded QR codes.

👥Contacts

Full contact management for suppliers and customers with order history.

📊Reports

Inventory value, movement history, dead stock. Export to PDF or Excel.

🔔Stock Alerts

Automatic low-stock and out-of-stock notifications.

📄Audit Trail

Every movement logged — receipts, shipments, transfers, adjustments.

❒Barcode & QR

Generate and download barcodes or QR codes for any product.

☁Cloud Sync

Writes to local SQLite instantly. Syncs to Supabase in the background.

🔒Role-Based Access

Admin, Manager, Staff, and Viewer roles with fine-grained permissions.

Architecture
⚡
Offline-first writes
Every action writes to local SQLite first — the UI responds instantly with no network latency.
☁
Background sync
Changes are queued and pushed to Supabase (Postgres) in the background, typically within 30 seconds of coming online.
⚒
Conflict resolution
On pull, remote state wins for rows not in the local sync queue — ensures consistency without data loss.
📷
Local image protocol
Images stored in userData/product-images/ and served via a custom local-image:// protocol — no external hosting needed.
